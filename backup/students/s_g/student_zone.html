Documents Title

It is hereby notified for information of all concerned that those who are eligible for re-admission in the Spring Semester 2010 are required to get re-admitted by paying necessary fees at the very beginning of the semester so that they do not miss any theoretical/sessional classes. This is absolutely necessary to cover up their academic deficiency and for appropriate evaluation of their classroom performances.

Therefore, those who are eligible for re-admission are asked to take re-admission immediately, but not later than 29 April, 2010 by which all the formalities including the payment of re-admission fees must be completed. This last date for re-admission will not be extended under any circumstances.
