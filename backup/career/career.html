Assistant Accounts Officer (AAO):

Requirements: The candidates must have at least Masters Degree in Commerce/Business Administration or its equivalent from any recognized university/ institution with minimum 2nd Division/Class at all stages. They must have at least 5 years experience in accounts in any reputed organization preferably in any higher level educational institution. They must have good knowledge in English and computer skills.
